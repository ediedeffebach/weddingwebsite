For family and friends, from Portland to Peckham, we've put together a few of our favourite London spots - beyond all the 'must-dos' you can get elsewhere. If you're after the perfect day in Hackney, we recommend starting out at Violet Cafe and talking a stroll over to Broadway Market, if its a Saturday or Columbia Road Flower Market, if it's a Sunday. On a nice day, take a walk along Regents Canal to London Fields or Victoria Park. For restaurants in the area, we love Bright, Rawduck and Lardo. In Dalston, check out delicious, cheap ethiopian food at Andu Cafe and visit Dalston Curve Gardenfor a hidden pocket of nature.

Further west on Regents Canal, we recommend Toconoco or Towpath Cafe for lunch. For drinks, Edie's favourite bar is 3 Sheets and Lorcan's is Brilliant Corners. Our favourite Islington restaurants are Trullo and Ottolenghi - if you're after a Sunday Roast, the Snooty Fox or the Smokehouse are delicious. For a bit of theatre, see what's on at the Almeida (in Islignton) or the National Theatre (Southbank). The TodayTix App is a good way to get some discounted theatre tickets.

If it's your first time in London, we really recommend going to the Southbank, just off of Waterloo. From there you can go to the Tate Modern, the Hayward Gallery, Globe Theatre and also grab a bite to eat at Borough Market by Tower Bridge. Over the river and further East, you'll be a walk away from Trafalgar Square, Covent Garden and the National Gallery. Hampstead Heath is also wonderful in the Summer - you can take a dip in the ponds if you're feeling brave!

Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions or want more recommendations!

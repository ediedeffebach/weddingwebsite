Lorcan and Edie (or Erica, if you knew her before 2011) are very much looking forward to sharing their wedding with you at the Barbican Centre Conservatory. Please arrive between 3:00 & 3:30pm. The Barbican is a beautiful and iconic estate - for those who haven't been before, we reccommend finding some time to have a look around. There will of course be plenty of time to take in the bountiful, botanic beauty of the conservatory before, during, and after the ceremony.

There will be a cocktail reception, wine with the meal, and a champagne toast provided, as well as a cash bar. Please indicate if you'd like a vegetarian option or have any dietary restrictions in your RSVP. Dress code is semi-casual and ties are optional. We sadly have a 23:30 curfew but there are loads of good clubs nearby in Shoreditch, Farringdon, and, our neck of the woods, Dalston.

The nearest tube station is the eponymoulsy named 'Barbican', which can be reached by the Circle, Hammersmith & City, and Metropolitan lines. For the 'die-hards', the nearest night tube would be the Central Line from St Paul's. For those outside of London, we reccommend getting the CityMapper App. to help get you around town on public transport and Uber is always and option. The Barbican and Conservatory is wheelchair accessible.
